Being Modern

Domestic Chemical Products and Manifestations of Modernity

Along with specialised mechanical technologies which modernised home life, new household chemical products were marketed along with them. Softer hands, more hygienic surfaces, less backache in the garden and even better relationships were all promised by chemical products for household chores. Taking advantage of these new chemicals was the modern thing to do.
