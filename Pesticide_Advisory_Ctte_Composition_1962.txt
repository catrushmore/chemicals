HANSARD 1803–2005 → 1960s → 1962 → April 1962 → 16 April 1962 → Written Answers (Commons) → AGRICULTURE, FISHERIES AND FOOD
Advisory Committee on Poisonous Substances

HC Deb 16 April 1962 vol 658 cc15-6W 15W
§Mr. Farr asked the Minister of Agriculture, Fisheries and Food if he will list the names of those who form the Government's Advisory Committee on Poisonous Substances.
Mr. Vane The Advisory Committee on Poisonous Substances Used in Agriculture and Food Storage is at present constituted as follows:
Chairman

Sir Charles Dodds, M.V.O., F.R.S., D.Sc., Ph.D., M.D., F.R.C.P., F.R.I.C., F.R.S.E., Courtauld Professor of Biochemistry in the University of London and Director of the Courtauld Institute of Biochemistry, Middlesex Hospital.
