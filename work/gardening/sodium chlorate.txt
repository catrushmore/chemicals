uses - 95% sodium chlorate is in the production of chlorine dioxide for bleaching in the pulp and paper industry. 
superior brightness over pulps bleached with other regains, cellulose not degraded so a white and stronger paper results. Chlorine dioxide cannot be shaped so is generated by the pulp producers. 
3% in 1990 was used as intermediate in the production of other chlorates and per chlorates
agricultural application 7000 metric tons in 1990 - herbicide, defoliant for bottom. Magnesium chlorate used as desiccant for soybeans - remove leaves.
