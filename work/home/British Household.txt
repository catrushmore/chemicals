The British Household

PG Gray
based on an inquiry April 1947

definitions
household - group of people living in same dwelling and sharin gin the same housekepeing unit. Boarders are coutned as members of the household, but people living as lodger in rooms and doing their own housekeeping are treated as separate households. 

housewife: the woman in the household mainly responsible for the housekeeping. She may, of course, go out to work in addition to this.
